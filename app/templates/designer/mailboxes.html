Mailboxes (email-to-ticket)

Poll an IMAP inbox and turn replies into ticket updates: a subject containing a ticket number (INC-0007 — recognized from every auto-number prefix — or an alias like I-7) becomes a public comment from the matched sender, with attachments saved to the record. Unknown or unauthorized senders land as internal guest notes (never shown in the portal). Numberless mail from a known sender can create a new ticket via the chosen catalog form. Outgoing comment emails carry [INC-0007] subjects so plain replies round-trip. Note: sender matching is by From address — convenience-grade identity, which is why unauthenticated content only ever becomes an internal note.
